Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch study plans mid-semester?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan instantly via your student dashboard account settings. Changes are pro-rated.
How does the Pass Guarantee work?
If you complete 90% of your customized study blueprint, sit for all diagnostic mock exams, and still do not pass your official licensing board, we refund up to 3 months of subscription fees.
How long do I keep dashboard analytics data?
You retain dashboard metrics for as long as your membership is active. Once cancelled, your weak area analytics and historical mock scores are saved for 6 months should you choose to return.
Are the tutors actual nurse educators?
Absolutely. Every tutor and curriculum planner is a verified nursing educator holding an active MSN or DNP, ensuring rationales conform to current licensing boards.
